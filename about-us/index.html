Who We Are

SNACITO wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born out of sheer hunger. We noticed a distinct lack of premium, delicious, and easy-to-eat midnight snacks on campus, especially during those intense study and coding sessions. We are students, food lovers, and night owls dedicated to bringing the best culinary comforts right to where you need them.

The Spardha Connection

Hackathons and good food go hand-in-hand. When you're 24 hours into a 36-hour sprint, staring at lines of code, you need more than just caffeine. You need sustenance that brings joy. That's why SNACITO partnered with Spardha. We are here to be your culinary pit-stop on your odyssey from Myths to Mainframes.

Our Promise

  • Fresh Ingredients: We don't compromise. Every waffle, every puff is made with love and quality stuff.
  • Midnight Delivery: We serve when you need us the most, ensuring your late-night cravings are always sorted.
  • Student-First: Built by students, for students. We understand the budget, the vibe, and the hunger.
